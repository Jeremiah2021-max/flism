Build a production-ready full-stack mobile fintech application called Flism for Ghana. Flism is a student-focused collateral-based loan platform that provides small, secure loans to university students using physical or digital assets as collateral.

The application must be modern, scalable, secure, mobile-first, and built with clean architecture and professional UI/UX similar to modern fintech and banking apps.

CORE BUSINESS MODEL

Flism operates primarily as a collateral-backed student microloan platform.

Initial launch strategy:

“Physical Collateral + Small Loans”
No unsecured loans for new users.
New borrowers must deposit physical collateral before loan approval.
Loan amounts should remain small initially to reduce fraud and financial risk.

Long-term scaling model:

Hybrid collateral system.
Trusted users may access digital collateral or reduced verification.
High-risk or new users must always provide physical collateral.

Example:

trusted returning borrower → phone verification only.
new borrower → physical collateral mandatory.
TARGET USERS
University students in Ghana
Young adults with smartphones and Mobile Money accounts
Students needing short-term emergency loans
