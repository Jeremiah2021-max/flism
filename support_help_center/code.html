Frequently Asked Questions

How do I release my collateral? expand_more
Collateral is automatically released back to your vault once the total loan amount and accrued interest have been paid in full. You will receive a confirmation GH₵ receipt immediately.
What are the interest rates? expand_more
Interest rates vary based on your student profile and historical repayment data. Typically, rates start at 2.5% per month for verified university accounts.
Can I pay in local GH₵? expand_more
Yes, Flism supports all major mobile money providers in Ghana. You can repay using MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, or direct bank transfer in GH₵.
